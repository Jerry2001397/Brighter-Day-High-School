About Us

Learn more about Brighter Day ELEM, Jr & Sr High School

Our Story

Brighter Day ELEM, Jr & Sr High School, located in the heart of Sou-Clinc Community, Paynesville, has been a beacon of educational excellence for many years. We are dedicated to nurturing young minds and preparing students for a bright future through quality education and character development.

Our Mission

Working to ensure that the kids get quality education at an affordable cost.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution in Liberia, recognized for academic excellence, innovation, and producing well-rounded graduates who make positive contributions to society.
